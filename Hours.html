Directions from the north:
Take I-95 South/Maine Turnpike. At exit 19, take the ramp on the right. Turn left on Rte.9/Rte.109 heading toward Wells. At Wells corner turn right onto U.S. 1 heading toward Ogunquit. Pass through the center of Ogunquit and continue until you reach Obeds Lane. Turn left onto Obeds Lane. The Museum is the first building on the left.

Directions from the south:
Take I-95 North/Maine Turnpike. At exit 7 take the ramp on the right and head toward U.S. Route 1 Turn left onto U.S. 1 heading toward Ogunquit. Before you reach the center of Ogunquit, turn right onto Obeds Lane. The Museum is the first building on the left.
