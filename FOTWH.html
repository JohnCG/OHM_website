The Friends of the Ogunquit Heritage Museum is a non-profit 501(c)³ corporation working to provide financial support to the Ogunquit Heritage Museum.  The Friends provide ongoing support to the museum’s educational programs and exhibits.  In the fall and spring of each year the Friends publish a newsletter.  These are mailed to our members and are also available on line and at the Museum.
